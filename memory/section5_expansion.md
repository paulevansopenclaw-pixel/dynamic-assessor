# Section 5: Sediment Control Expansion

## Overview
Section 5 covers the physical barriers and structures used to trap sediment.

## Planned Modules
1. **SD 5-1: Straw Bale Filter**
   - Symptoms: Water bypassing, bales rotting, sediment underflow.
   - Specs: Bales must be embedded 100mm into ground; 2 pickets per bale.
2. **SD 5-5: Mesh and Aggregate Filter**
   - Symptoms: Blocked aggregate, water backing up onto property.
   - Specs: 25-50mm aggregate; 100mm high.
3. **SD 5-3: Earth Basin (Type D/F)**
   - Symptoms: Turbid water, overflow during small rain events.
   - Specs: Settlement zone vs. Storage zone volume ratios.
